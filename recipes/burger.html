Cheeseburger

Burger Recipe

A cheeseburger recipe

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Buns: soft and white
  • Cheese
  • Green and Pickled stuff
    • lettuce
    • onions
    • tomatoes
    • Optional: pickles
  • The Sauce
    • Ketchup & Mayonnaise mix

Recipe Instructions

  1. Form the patties by grabbing a bit of beef and roll each into a ball and then press it down. Add salt before cooking.
  2. Preheat an iron skillet on high heat until very hot, place 2 or 4 patties and press down and cooke one side for 1.5 minutes
  3. Flip patty to other side, place cheese on top and cook for another 1.5 minutes
  4. Remove the patties with cheese, then toast/cook the buns a bit.
  5. Smear the buns with the sauce you made. Add the lettuce, tomatoes then patty with the buns
  6. Then you can add onions or pickles if you want. ENJOY!!
