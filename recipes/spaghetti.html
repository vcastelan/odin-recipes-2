Spaghetti Recipe

A Spaghetti recipe with meatballs

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Sauce
  • Pasta

Recipe Instructions

  1. Add salt and oil to a pan. boil hot water in the pan until boiling hot
  2. Add your pasta on the pan until its stringy.
  3. Drain the pot of boiling water and then leave on hot stove.
  4. Cook the ground beef on a seperate pan until hot and add the sauce to it.
  5. Finally add cooked beef to the pasta and stir. Add more sauce if needed. ENJOY!!
